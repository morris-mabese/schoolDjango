Fees Structure Requirements

The school operates a clear and transparent fees structure that is provided to parents and guardians upon pupil registration. This ensures that all families are well informed about tuition fees and any related charges before learning begins. The fees are structured to support quality education, learning materials, and the overall welfare of the pupils, while remaining fair and manageable for parents.
