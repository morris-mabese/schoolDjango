About Us

St. Anthony’s Little Angels Education Centre is a warm, nurturing, and faith-inspired learning community dedicated to the holistic development of young children. We believe that every child is a precious gift with unique talents, abilities, and potential. Our centre provides a safe and joyful environment where children are encouraged to learn, explore, and grow with confidence.

Guided by strong moral values and a child-centered approach, we focus on developing the whole child—academically, socially, emotionally, spiritually, and physically. Through play-based and structured learning experiences, we help children build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, creativity, and critical thinking while fostering kindness, respect, and responsibility.

At St. Anthony’s Little Angels, our caring and qualified educators are passionate about early childhood education. They create engaging lessons that recognize each child’s individual learning pace and style, ensuring that every learner feels supported and valued. We emphasize positive guidance, encouragement, and love to help children develop self-confidence and independence. We also believe that strong partnerships with parents and guardians are essential to a child’s success. By working closely with families, we create a supportive community that nurtures children both at school and at home.
